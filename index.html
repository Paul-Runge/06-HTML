Student Bio

Paul Runge

My name is Paul. I grew up in Baton Rouge, LA. I left high school in my junior year and obtained my GED instead. That very summer I entered college, but did not enjoy it very much. Seeing as how I had started in the culinary field while still in highschool, I persued a career as a chef. I worked at various establishments around SE Louisiana and briefly in tiny town in western North Carolina before return to Baton Rouge. North Carolina was beautiful, and I really hated to leave.

For as long as I can remember, I've wanted to travel. In the summer of 2016 I quit my job and toured around the US for several months. At times it was nerve wracking, but ultimately very rewarding. I saw some amazing sights, camped in scenic forests, and met some unique and intersting people. After spending a week reconecting with some old friends living in Portland, I decided to move there. I spent nearly 2 years saving money, then left town with whatever I could fit in my car heading to the PNW. That was nearly five years ago. I really enjoy living here. With easy access to great food, the ocean, and all the hiking one could dream of, I can't imagine living anywhere else.
